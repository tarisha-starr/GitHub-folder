# Is the second photo the end of the story?

**A couples retreat for the long-married. Wanaka. April 2027.**

---

There's a photo of us from before kids.

His arm around me, I'm looking at him with love, sun behind us turning everything gold. So much happiness and joy! Gives me goosebumps...

I look at it sometimes and I don't recognise the woman in it. Or the man. Or the way they're looking at each other.

And then I look at us now. The photo nobody takes because nobody thinks to.

Two people on a sofa. Phones out. Tired.

Is this familiar?

Here's what I know.

The second photo is not the end of your story. It's just a chapter where you both forgot you were the main characters.
