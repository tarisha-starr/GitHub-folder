# Hook Rules

The hook is 80% of growth. If the first line doesn't stop the scroll, nothing
else matters. These rules apply at every level (1, 2, 3) and to every post.

## Templates I rotate

- 🚨 BREAKING: [bold claim]
- STOP [common mistake]
- R.I.P [old way] 💀
- NO WAYYY 🤯
- 99% of people don't know this
- I [result] in [time]. Here's how
- YOU + [tool] = [outcome]

## Rules

- Under 8 words.
- Works as a standalone sentence.
- If it doesn't make someone think "wait what?" — delete it.
- Write 15 to 20 hooks before choosing one.
- Negativity and curiosity win.
- No soft openers ("hey guys", "so today I want to…", "I just wanted to share…").
- No advice voice ("you should…", "try…").
- No clinical voice ("studies show…", "research suggests…").
- No performative empathy ("I see you, queen").

## Niche-specific bank

For the midlife-women / desire / intimacy niche, see `content/hooks.md` —
that's the running bank of niche-tuned hook openers. Use the templates above
for structure, the bank for tone.

## Generation prompt

Use this prompt verbatim when generating hooks for a new batch:

> You are a viral hook writer. You studied 10,000+ posts that got millions of
> views. My niche is **[NICHE]**. Generate 30 hooks under 8 words. Use bold
> claims, pattern interrupts, curiosity gaps, death statements, and secret
> reveals. Rank them from highest viral potential to lowest.

Then filter to the top 10–15 that also fit the niche tone in `content/hooks.md`.
