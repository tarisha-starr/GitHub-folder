# Hormozi Offer Builder — Reusable Template

Use this template to engineer any new offer through the Hormozi $100M framework. Fill in the INPUT section, then work through each step. The output is a high-converting, scalable offer with clear positioning, pricing logic, and irresistible value.

Paste the entire template (with your inputs filled in) into Claude or ChatGPT along with ME.md for voice and brand context.

---

**Pain level:** [what frustrates them, costs them money, keeps them up at night]

**Desired outcome:** [the specific transformation they want — be concrete]

**Market sophistication:** [beginner / aware / saturated — how much have they already tried?]

## STEP 1: Identify the Highest Urgency Problem

What is the ONE problem this audience will pay to solve right now?

- Not the problem you think is most important. The one THEY feel most urgently.
- Test: would they search for this at 2am? Would they tell a friend about it?
- Evidence of willingness to pay: what are they already spending money on that circles around this problem?

**Write it here:**
> [The urgent problem in one sentence]

---

## STEP 2: Quantify the Cost of NOT Solving

Make the price of inaction concrete. Fill in all three:

| Dimension | Cost of doing nothing |
|---|---|
| **Time** | [What do they lose in months/years if they don't fix this?] |
| **Money** | [What are they spending on half-solutions, or what will they spend if this gets worse?] |
| **Stress / Identity** | [What does it cost their confidence, relationships, sense of self?] |

**The "do nothing" price tag:** $[estimate] over [timeframe] — this number must be significantly higher than your offer price.

---

## STEP 3: Design the Core Offer

The core offer delivers the fastest, most tangible result. Not everything you could teach — the ONE thing that moves the needle fastest.

**Core promise:** [In one sentence: what do they get, and how fast?]

**Delivery:**
- Format: [how they receive it]
- Duration: [how long]
- Frequency: [daily / weekly / intensive]
- Curriculum outline:
  - Phase 1: [what + outcome]
  - Phase 2: [what + outcome]
  - Phase 3: [what + outcome]

## STEP 4: Stack Value Aggressively

Each element must increase perceived value. If it doesn't, cut it.

### Speed (how fast they win)
- [What result can they feel by Day 1 / Week 1?]
- [What will others notice, and when?]

### Certainty (guarantees + proof)
- **Guarantee:** [What do you promise if it doesn't work? Make it specific and generous.]
- **Proof:** [Testimonials, numbers, credentials — list everything you have]
- **Container size:** [Small group = personal attention = higher certainty]

### Support (done-with-you, access)
- [Live calls / community / direct access / check-ins — what level of support?]
- [Pre-work or onboarding that makes them feel held from Day 0]

### Bonuses (remove friction points)
Each bonus should solve a specific objection or friction point. Name the friction, then the bonus.

| Friction point | Bonus that removes it | Perceived value |
|---|---|---|
| [e.g., "partner won't understand"] | [e.g., Partner Conversation Guide] | [$X] |
| [friction 2] | [bonus 2] | [$X] |
| [friction 3] | [bonus 3] | [$X] |
| [friction 4] | [bonus 4] | [$X] |

**Total perceived value:** $[sum all elements]
**Actual price:** $[your price]
**Value-to-price ratio:** [should be 10:1 or higher]

## STEP 5: Rewrite the Offer for 5-Second Clarity

The offer must be:
- Extremely clear
- Outcome-driven (not feature-driven)
- Understandable in under 5 seconds

**Headline:** [One line. The transformation, not the method.]

**Subhead:** [Who it's for + what changes.]

**Price:** $[X]
**Payment plan:** [if applicable]
**Guarantee:** [one line]

**Test:** Read the headline to someone who knows nothing about your work. Do they immediately understand what they get? If not, rewrite.
