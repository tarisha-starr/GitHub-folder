# Prompt 1: Retention Audit

---

Act like a ruthless content retention strategist.

I will give you my content.

Your job is to find every moment where the audience might get bored, confused, or leave.

Analyze:

1. Hook strength
2. First 3 seconds
3. Curiosity gap
4. Pacing
5. Repetition
6. Emotional pull
7. Payoff
8. Ending

Give me a clear score from 1 to 10 and tell me exactly what to fix.
