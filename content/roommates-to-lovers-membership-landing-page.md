## SECTION 11, FAQ

**Do I need to have done the 5 days first?**
Yes. The membership picks up where your Desire Map left off. If you haven't done the 5 days, [start there](/find-each-other-again) and come back.

**My partner doesn't know I'm doing this. Is that a problem?**
Not at all. The membership is your work, on your time. Most members keep the calls and the practices private. Many partners notice the shift weeks before they're told the source of it.

**Can I just do one month?**
Yes. Cancel after 30 days in two clicks if it's not landing. No questions, no guilt. The women who stay three months or more are the ones who say it changed everything. The shift is cumulative.

**What if I miss a live call?**
Every call is recorded and posted the next morning. Watch it with a cup of tea on Saturday. Two calls per month means you've got a second chance built in.

**Is this the Radiant Woman Circle?**
The membership and the Circle share DNA: somatic practice, nervous system, desire, pleasure, voice. The Radiant Woman Circle is the in-person, deep-immersion container. The membership is the monthly practice room. Many women do both. You don't have to.

**Is this religious or spiritual in any way?**
No specific framework. The work is grounded in psychology and somatic practice. Your beliefs are welcome and never required.

**What if I'm in peri or menopause?**
That's exactly why this works. Your rules have changed. The membership helps you keep mapping what your body needs now, not what worked at 30.

**What if I'm single, divorced, or widowed?**
The map is yours, first. The practices work regardless of partnership status. You're welcome in the room.

**How long do I have access?**
For as long as you're a member. The archive grows. Your past practices stay yours while you're in. The day you cancel, you stop paying. Simple.

**What does "founding member" actually mean?**
You're in the room the day it opens. You lock in $97/month for life. When the price moves to $147 for new joiners, your rate doesn't change for as long as you stay. If you cancel and re-join later, you join at the current rate, so the lock holds while you're in.

**I'm already in the $47/mo Club. What happens to me?**
The Club is closing. You're invited in as a founding member at $97/month. The new room has more (two live calls instead of one, the Signal community, the Reclaim Your Erotic Power retreat first-access). You'll get a separate email walking you through it, no action needed today.

**Do I need Signal? I've never used it.**
Free app, two-minute install, works on any phone. We pick Signal because the conversations in this room aren't for Facebook to see. We'll walk you through setup on day one.

**What if sex hurts, or I have trauma?**
The membership is practice, not therapy. If anything in a session activates you, pause and prioritise support. If you have unresolved sexual trauma, please work with a trauma-trained therapist alongside the membership.

**Is this just for women in couples?**
No. It started in the couples world (the 5 days helps women in long relationships find their desire again), and many members are partnered. Plenty aren't. The map is about your relationship with your own body first. That work doesn't need a partner.
