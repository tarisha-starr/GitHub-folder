# Hook Rewrites: 20 Hooks x 10 Angles

**Every core hook rewritten through 10 scroll-stopping angles.**
Each version is short, human, easy to understand, and designed for maximum 3-second hold rate.

**The 10 angles:**
1. **Shock** ... hits hard, makes her stop mid-scroll
2. **Curiosity** ... opens a gap she has to close
3. **Pain** ... names the wound directly
4. **Speed** ... promises a fast, specific result
5. **Mistake** ... names what she's doing wrong (compassionately)
6. **Before/After** ... shows the shift
7. **Hidden trick** ... insider knowledge she doesn't have yet
8. **Contrarian** ... goes against what everyone else says
9. **Simple promise** ... one clear outcome
10. **Money** ... reframed as "cost" (emotional, relational, time lost)
