# 30 Educational Reels: Evidence, Tips & Practical Advice

**Purpose:** Teach, inform, and give practical tools while keeping Tarisha's voice
**Format:** Each reel leads with feeling, teaches in the middle, closes with action
**Rule:** Every fact earns its place by connecting to something she FEELS. No dry lectures.
**Curiosity:** 3 touches per reel (hook gap, mid-point reset, close loop)
