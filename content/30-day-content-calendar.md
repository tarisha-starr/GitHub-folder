# 30-Day Instagram Viral Content Calendar ... Tarisha Starr

**Niche:** Women over 40 | Desire, intimacy, body reconnection, midlife
**Goal:** Maximum views on a new page using proven viral patterns

---

## Week 1: "She Feels Caught" (Identity + Recognition)

### Day 1, Monday
- **Hook:** "You're not broken. You're exhausted."
- **Content angle:** Name the burnout she's been calling a personal failure
- **Format:** Reel (15s), text on screen with soft b-roll of a woman sitting quietly
- **Why they stop scrolling:** The word "broken" triggers instant recognition. She's said this about herself.
- **Shareability:** She sends this to her best friend who's been saying "I don't know what's wrong with me"
- **CTA:** "Comment 'exhausted' if this hits home"

### Day 2, Tuesday
- **Hook:** "The loneliest place can be next to someone you love."
- **Content angle:** Emotional distance in long-term relationships isn't about love dying
- **Format:** Carousel (4 slides), hook → the feeling → why it happens → what to do first
- **Why they stop scrolling:** It names a specific pain she's never said out loud
- **Shareability:** She saves it and sends it to her partner with "this is what I've been trying to say"
- **CTA:** "Save this if you needed to hear it"

### Day 3, Wednesday
- **Hook:** "I had a client say 'I think I want more'... like it was a confession."
- **Content angle:** Women over 40 have been trained to feel guilty for wanting pleasure, closeness, themselves
- **Format:** Reel (30s), talking head, direct to camera, intimate tone
- **Why they stop scrolling:** The word "confession" creates curiosity. What did the client say?
- **Shareability:** The idea that wanting more is normal (not selfish) is deeply shareable among women in this stage
- **CTA:** "What would you ask for if you weren't afraid?"

### Day 4, Thursday
- **Hook:** "When did you stop choosing yourself?"
- **Content angle:** The slow disappearance of self that happens through years of caregiving
- **Format:** Image post, woman in soft light, hook as text overlay
- **Why they stop scrolling:** The question is direct and personal. She can't scroll past without answering it internally.
- **Shareability:** High save rate. Women bookmark this as a reminder.
- **CTA:** "Say with me: 'I choose me'"

### Day 5, Friday
- **Hook:** "Your body didn't betray you. She adapted."
- **Content angle:** Menopause, low desire, and shutdown aren't failures. They're intelligent responses.
- **Format:** Reel (20s), text on screen with slow, grounding music
- **Why they stop scrolling:** Reframes her deepest fear (my body is broken) into compassion
- **Shareability:** Women tag friends going through perimenopause. High share-to-DM rate.
- **CTA:** "Comment 'her' if you're ready to listen to your body again"

### Day 6, Saturday
- **Hook:** "Things that are normal but shouldn't be: ignoring your body for 20 years and wondering why she shut down."
- **Content angle:** Pattern interrupt. Use a trending audio or meme-style format.
- **Format:** Reel (10-15s), trending audio + bold text on screen
- **Why they stop scrolling:** The format feels casual/fun but the content hits deep
- **Shareability:** Trending format = algorithm push. Relatable truth = organic shares.
- **CTA:** "Share this with someone who needs to hear it"

### Day 7, Sunday
- **Hook:** "Tonight, put your hand on your belly and say: 'How are you doing, darling?'"
- **Content angle:** Micro-practice for body reconnection. Simple, doable, emotional.
- **Format:** Carousel (3 slides), the practice → why it works → what might come up
- **Why they stop scrolling:** It's an invitation, not a lecture. The word "darling" disarms.
- **Shareability:** Women save practices. This is a "come back to this Sunday night" post.
- **CTA:** "Save this for tonight. Tell me what comes up."

## Week 2: "She Starts to Trust You" (Depth + Authority)

### Day 8, Monday
- **Hook:** "You don't hate sex. You hate pressure."
- **Content angle:** Low desire isn't about sex. It's about the dynamic around sex.
- **Format:** Reel (20s), direct to camera, slow pacing, one idea only
- **Why they stop scrolling:** She's been blaming herself for this. You just told her it's not her fault.
- **Shareability:** Partners need to see this. High "sent to partner" rate.
- **CTA:** "Comment 'pressure' if this resonates"

### Day 9, Tuesday
- **Hook:** "The spark didn't die. It got buried under years of being useful."
- **Content angle:** Desire, passion, and aliveness don't disappear. They get deprioritised.
- **Format:** Image post, warm-toned, woman in evening light, hook as overlay
- **Why they stop scrolling:** "Being useful" is a phrase every woman in this audience feels in her bones
- **Shareability:** Saves. She wants to remember this.
- **CTA:** "What got buried for you?"

### Day 10, Wednesday
- **Hook:** "3 things your body is telling you that you've been ignoring"
- **Content angle:** Tension, fatigue, shutdown, these are messages, not malfunctions
- **Format:** Carousel (5 slides), hook → 3 signs → what to do → invitation
- **Why they stop scrolling:** Numbered lists create completion drive. She needs to see all 3.
- **Shareability:** Educational + personal = save-heavy content
- **CTA:** "Which one hit hardest? Drop the number below."

### Day 11, Thursday
- **Hook:** "She said 'I love him, so why do I feel so alone?' I hear this every single week."
- **Content angle:** The gap between love and connection in long-term relationships
- **Format:** Reel (45s), storytelling format, client scenario (anonymised), talking head
- **Why they stop scrolling:** The quote sounds like something she's said herself. The "every single week" adds social proof.
- **Shareability:** She screenshots the hook and sends it to her group chat
- **CTA:** "If this is you... you're not alone. Reach out."

### Day 12, Friday
- **Hook:** "Before you blame your body... read this."
- **Content angle:** Low desire, weight changes, exhaustion. These aren't failures. They're signals.
- **Format:** Carousel (4 slides), the blame → the truth → the reframe → the invitation
- **Why they stop scrolling:** "Read this" creates an open loop. She has to swipe.
- **Shareability:** Women forward this to other women who are in the "blaming" stage
- **CTA:** "Save this for someone who needs it"

### Day 13, Saturday
- **Hook:** "Unpopular opinion: most 'low desire' isn't low desire. It's a smart body protecting herself."
- **Content angle:** Controversial reframe that makes her rethink everything she's been told
- **Format:** Reel (15s), bold text on screen, strong music, mic-drop energy
- **Why they stop scrolling:** "Unpopular opinion" + "smart body" = two hooks in one. Debate-worthy.
- **Shareability:** Polarising content gets shared in DMs and commented on heavily
- **CTA:** "Agree or disagree? Tell me below."

### Day 14, Sunday
- **Hook:** "This week, ask yourself one question every morning: 'What does my body need today?'"
- **Content angle:** Week-long micro-practice. Sets up a ritual.
- **Format:** Image post, calm visual, journal-style prompt, minimalist design
- **Why they stop scrolling:** It's gentle and actionable. Sunday = planning mode.
- **Shareability:** Women save weekly challenges and journal prompts
- **CTA:** "Screenshot this and put it by your bed."

## Week 3: "She Comes Back Every Day" (Connection + Depth)

### Day 15, Monday
- **Hook:** "Everything looks fine. That's the part that hurts."
- **Content angle:** The invisible pain of functional relationships that lack emotional intimacy
- **Format:** Reel (20s), text on screen, couple sitting at dinner, soft b-roll
- **Why they stop scrolling:** It names the paradox she lives in. Nothing is wrong AND everything is wrong.
- **Shareability:** Extremely high. This is the post she DMs to her friend saying "this is my marriage"
- **CTA:** "Comment 'fine' if you know this feeling"

### Day 16, Tuesday
- **Hook:** "One honest sentence can change the whole room."
- **Content angle:** The power of vulnerability in a relationship that's gone quiet
- **Format:** Carousel (5 slides), hook → what usually happens → what to try → example sentences → invitation
- **Why they stop scrolling:** Hope. She wants to believe one sentence could shift things.
- **Shareability:** Practical + hopeful = saved AND shared
- **CTA:** "What's the one sentence you've been holding back?"

### Day 17, Wednesday
- **Hook:** "You don't need to become someone else to be desired."
- **Content angle:** The myth that she needs to be thinner, younger, more adventurous to be wanted
- **Format:** Reel (25s), direct to camera, soft, intimate delivery
- **Why they stop scrolling:** It directly contradicts the story diet culture and media have told her for decades
- **Shareability:** Body-positive reframes get shared widely in women's communities
- **CTA:** "Comment 'enough' if you're done performing"

### Day 18, Thursday
- **Hook:** "Last week a woman told me she can't remember the last time she felt beautiful. Not dressed-up beautiful. Just... her."
- **Content angle:** The loss of self-recognition in midlife
- **Format:** Reel (40s), storytelling, talking head, emotional arc
- **Why they stop scrolling:** The specificity of "not dressed-up beautiful, just... her", she's never heard anyone say this
- **Shareability:** This is a "tag someone" post. Women tag their friends who need to hear this.
- **CTA:** "When did you last feel like YOU?"

### Day 19, Friday
- **Hook:** "Maybe you're not tired of sex. Maybe you're tired of performing."
- **Content angle:** The difference between desire and performance. Sexual scripts vs authentic expression.
- **Format:** Image post, woman in bathrobe, reflective mood, hook overlay
- **Why they stop scrolling:** "Performing" is the word she's never used but immediately recognises
- **Shareability:** This reframe gets shared in private group chats constantly
- **CTA:** "What would intimacy look like if you stopped performing?"

### Day 20, Saturday
- **Hook:** "POV: you stopped trying to fix yourself and started listening instead."
- **Content angle:** Aspirational. Show the shift. Use trending POV format.
- **Format:** Reel (15s), POV trend, soft visuals, text on screen, trending audio
- **Why they stop scrolling:** POV format is pattern-recognisable + the content subverts expectations
- **Shareability:** Aspirational + trending format = reach play
- **CTA:** "Save this as a reminder"

### Day 21, Sunday
- **Hook:** "Write this down: 'I don't have to earn rest. I don't have to earn pleasure. I don't have to earn love.'"
- **Content angle:** Journal prompt + affirmation hybrid. Permission-giving content.
- **Format:** Carousel (3 slides), the prompt → why it matters → space to sit with it
- **Why they stop scrolling:** The repetition of "I don't have to earn" hits rhythmically and emotionally
- **Shareability:** Screenshot-worthy. Women save this and write it in their journals.
- **CTA:** "Which one do you need most today?"

## Week 4: "She Tells Everyone About You" (Momentum + Virality)

### Day 22, Monday
- **Hook:** "The hidden cost of being the strong one."
- **Content angle:** Emotional labour, invisible load, burnout disguised as strength
- **Format:** Reel (30s), talking head, list the costs one by one, emotional build
- **Why they stop scrolling:** Every woman in this audience identifies as "the strong one"
- **Shareability:** Extremely high. She sends this to every woman she knows.
- **CTA:** "Comment 'me' if you're tired of being strong"

### Day 23, Tuesday
- **Hook:** "5 signs your body is begging you to slow down"
- **Content angle:** Physical symptoms of emotional disconnection (tension, insomnia, low desire, digestive issues, skin)
- **Format:** Carousel (7 slides), hook → 5 signs (one per slide) → what to do
- **Why they stop scrolling:** Numbered lists + body symptoms = high curiosity + completion drive
- **Shareability:** Save-heavy. Women come back to this when symptoms show up.
- **CTA:** "How many did you check off? Be honest."

### Day 24, Wednesday
- **Hook:** "This is what reconnection actually looks like. It's not candles and lingerie."
- **Content angle:** Debunk the Hollywood version. Real reconnection is a hand on a shoulder, a genuine question, eye contact.
- **Format:** Reel (30s), text on screen with real-life couple moments (b-roll or stock)
- **Why they stop scrolling:** It challenges the image she's been failing to live up to
- **Shareability:** Partners need to see this. "This is what I actually need" energy.
- **CTA:** "What does reconnection look like for you?"

### Day 25, Thursday
- **Hook:** "I stopped trying to want sex. I started trying to want myself. Everything shifted."
- **Content angle:** The link between self-desire and partnered desire
- **Format:** Reel (45s), personal/client story arc, talking head, emotional delivery
- **Why they stop scrolling:** The twist. She expects a sex tip. She gets a self-reclamation story.
- **Shareability:** Transformation stories are the most saved content format in wellness niches
- **CTA:** "Has your relationship with yourself changed how you show up in your relationship?"

### Day 26, Friday
- **Hook:** "You've been holding it together for so long you forgot what 'letting go' feels like."
- **Content angle:** Permission to release control, ask for help, fall apart a little
- **Format:** Image post, woman outdoors, wind in hair, soft light, hook overlay
- **Why they stop scrolling:** The word "forgot", she didn't choose this, she just... forgot
- **Shareability:** High tag rate. Women tag friends who "hold it together"
- **CTA:** "Who do you trust enough to let go with?"

### Day 27, Saturday
- **Hook:** "Things I'd tell every woman turning 40: you're not losing yourself. You're meeting yourself."
- **Content angle:** Reframe midlife as a beginning, not a decline. Use list or advice format.
- **Format:** Reel (20s), text on screen, aspirational music, one line at a time
- **Why they stop scrolling:** Age-specific content feels personal. "Meeting yourself" is an unexpected reframe.
- **Shareability:** Birthday post energy. Women share this at milestone ages.
- **CTA:** "What's one thing you've discovered about yourself after 40?"

### Day 28, Sunday
- **Hook:** "Breathe in for 4. Hold for 4. Exhale for 6. Repeat. You just told your nervous system: we're safe."
- **Content angle:** Micro-practice. Nervous system regulation. Ties back to desire, body, safety.
- **Format:** Reel (30s), guided practice, soft music, direct to camera or voiceover
- **Why they stop scrolling:** Interactive content (she breathes along). Immediate value.
- **Shareability:** Practice content is saved at 3-5x the rate of opinion content
- **CTA:** "Save this for when your body feels like it's on high alert"

### Day 29, Monday
- **Hook:** "The woman you miss is still in there."
- **Content angle:** She hasn't lost herself. She just buried herself under everyone else's needs.
- **Format:** Image post, woman smiling at herself in mirror, warm light, hook overlay
- **Why they stop scrolling:** Hope. Pure, simple hope. Seven words that hit like a full therapy session.
- **Shareability:** The highest-share hook in this niche. Women send this to each other constantly.
- **CTA:** "Comment 'still here' if you know she's in there"

### Day 30, Tuesday
- **Hook:** "This page is for the woman who loves deeply but has forgotten how to receive."
- **Content angle:** Community-building. Invitation post. Who this page is for.
- **Format:** Reel (30s), mission statement style, direct to camera, music builds
- **Why they stop scrolling:** She feels called out and called in at the same time
- **Shareability:** "Follow this page" energy. Women tag friends. This is your growth accelerator.
- **CTA:** "Follow if this is you. Share if you know her."

## Key Metrics to Track Per Post

For each of the 30 posts, log:
- Reach
- Saves
- Shares
- Comments
- Watch time (Reels)
- Profile visits
- Follows from post

After 30 days, identify the top 5 performers and create 5 variations of each. That's your next 25 posts.
