## 2. The Five Viral Triggers for This Niche

Every post must hit at least two of these.

### Trigger 1: "I thought I was the only one"
The audience has been carrying these feelings in silence. When you name it, she screenshots it, sends it to her friend, saves it for later. This is the #1 driver of shares in this niche.

**How to activate:** Name a specific, slightly embarrassing feeling with zero judgment.
- "You love him. And sometimes you lock the bathroom door just to breathe."
- "You don't hate sex. You hate being the one who has to want it."

### Trigger 2: "She just said what I've been thinking"
This is the authority trigger. You say the thing her therapist dances around. It positions you as the person who gets it.

**How to activate:** Use "I tell my clients..." or "I see this every day in my work..." followed by a reframe.

### Trigger 3: "I need to show this to my partner"
The share-with-partner trigger. Content that validates her experience AND gives language she can borrow.

**How to activate:** Frame the content around the relationship dynamic, not just her feeling. Use "he" or "your partner" so it reads like a bridge, not an attack.

### Trigger 4: "Wait, that's not what I expected"
The pattern interrupt. Most content in this niche is soft and affirming. When you open with something that sounds harsh and then reframe it, the algorithm rewards the watch-time spike.

**How to activate:** Open with a bold or slightly provocative statement. Pause. Then deliver the compassionate truth underneath.
- "Stop trying to fix your sex life." [pause] "Start listening to your body instead."

### Trigger 5: "I want to feel like that"
Aspirational desire. Show the after. Not the polished influencer version... the quiet, real version. A woman who looks relaxed in her body. A couple who laugh. A moment of genuine pleasure.

**How to activate:** Pair aspirational visuals with a single-line hook that names what she wants but hasn't said out loud.

## 3. Content Pillars (The Viral Mix)

Post from these five pillars in rotation. Each one serves a different algorithmic and audience function.

### Pillar 1: THE MIRROR (40% of content)
**Purpose:** Reach + shares + saves
**What it is:** You name her exact experience. She feels caught.
**Format:** Text-on-image, talking head with hook overlay, or carousel
**Examples:**
- "The loneliest place can be next to someone you love."
- "You've been holding everyone together. But who's holding you?"

### Pillar 2: THE REFRAME (25% of content)
**Purpose:** Authority + saves + comments
**What it is:** Take a belief she holds ("I'm broken," "I've lost my desire") and replace it with a deeper truth.
**Format:** Short Reel (15-30s), carousel with before/after belief, or single image
**Examples:**
- "You don't hate your body. You've stopped listening to her."
- "Desire doesn't come back through pressure. It comes back through safety."

### Pillar 3: THE STORY (15% of content)
**Purpose:** Watch time + retention + trust
**What it is:** A client story (anonymised), a personal story, or a "things I see in my work" narrative.
**Format:** Talking head Reel (30-60s), multi-slide carousel, or caption-heavy image post
**Examples:**
- "A client came to me and said 'I think I want more'... like it was a confession."
- "Last week a woman told me she can't remember the last time she felt beautiful. Not dressed up beautiful. Just... her."

### Pillar 4: THE PRACTICE (10% of content)
**Purpose:** Saves + DMs + email leads
**What it is:** A micro-practice, prompt, or exercise she can do right now.
**Format:** Carousel (step-by-step), Reel with guided instruction, or journal prompt image
**Examples:**
- "Tonight, put your hand on your belly and ask: 'How are you doing, darling?'"
- "3 questions to ask yourself before you say 'I'm fine'"

### Pillar 5: THE SPARK (10% of content)
**Purpose:** Shares + reach + new audience
**What it is:** A short, polarising, or trending-format post that introduces your world to new people.
**Format:** Trending audio Reel, meme-style text post, or hot take
**Examples:**
- Trending audio + text: "Things that are normal but shouldn't be: ignoring your body for 20 years and wondering why she shut down"
- "Unpopular opinion: most 'low desire' isn't low desire. It's a smart body protecting herself."

## 4. Posting Strategy

### Frequency
- **Minimum:** 5 posts per week (1/day weekdays)
- **Growth mode:** 7 posts per week (daily)
- **Aggressive growth:** 10-14 per week (1-2/day including Stories)

- **Best posting windows:** 6:00-7:30am, 12:00-1:00pm, 8:00-9:30pm (audience local time)

## 6. Shareability Engineering

### Why people share in this niche

| Reason | How to trigger it |
|---|---|
| "This IS me" | Ultra-specific emotional details (not generic affirmations) |
| "She needs to see this" | Content that validates + gives language, so she can forward it |
| "I can't say this myself" | You say the hard thing. She shares it as a proxy for her own voice. |
| "This is important" | Frame content as something hidden/taboo/under-discussed |

### Shareability checklist (run every post through this)
- [ ] Can she screenshot this and send it to one specific person?
- [ ] Does it name a feeling she hasn't been able to articulate?
- [ ] Would she save this to read again at 11pm?
- [ ] Does it make her feel less alone?
- [ ] Is there a phrase in here she'd want to borrow as her own words?

## 7. Retention and Watch-Time Tactics (for Reels)

1. **Open with the hook on screen in the first 0.5 seconds.** Don't wait.
2. **Use the "say less" principle.** 15-second Reels with one powerful idea outperform 60-second Reels that wander.
3. **Pattern interrupt at second 3-5.** Change the visual, add a text pop, shift tone.
4. **End with an open loop or question.** "But here's what she didn't expect..." or "Does this sound like you?"
5. **Rewatch triggers:** Say something so specific she replays it to make sure she heard it right.
6. **Caption hook ≠ Reel hook.** The caption should add a new layer, not repeat what's in the video.

## 8. Comment and DM Strategy

### Comment triggers (baked into every post)
- **Keyword CTAs:** "Comment 'me' if this is you" — drives comment count AND enables DM automation
- **This-or-that questions:** "Body or head... where do you live most?" — low barrier, high engagement
- **Confession prompts:** "When did you stop choosing yourself?" — vulnerable answers = longer comments = algorithm boost

### DM funnel
1. Keyword comment triggers automated DM (via ManyChat or similar)
2. DM delivers a free resource (journal prompt PDF, practice guide, quiz)
3. Resource leads to email list or discovery call booking
4. Every DM conversation is a chance to personally connect

## 9. Growth Accelerators

### Quick wins
- **Collaborate with complementary creators:** Pelvic floor physios, menopause nutritionists, couples therapists. Joint Reels or carousel takeovers.
- **Repurpose every post 3 ways:** Image post → Reel script → Story series → Email
- **Use the "1 idea, 5 formats" rule:** Take your best-performing hook and turn it into a Reel, carousel, image post, Story series, and email.
- **Comment on 20 accounts daily** in adjacent niches. Not spam. Real, thoughtful comments that make people tap your profile.

### Algorithm signals to prioritise
1. **Saves** — the #1 signal for reach in 2025-2026. Design for saves.
2. **Shares** — sends your content to new audiences. Design for "send to a friend."
3. **Watch time** — for Reels. Keep them short and re-watchable.
4. **Comments** — longer comments signal deep engagement. Ask questions that require real answers.
5. **Profile visits** — strong hooks + curiosity gaps drive profile taps.

Track weekly. Ignore vanity metrics (likes alone mean nothing).

| Metric | Target | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Saves per post | 50+ (scale with audience) | Best predictor of algorithmic reach |
| Shares per post | 20+ | Brings net-new audience |
| Reel watch time | 80%+ average | Signals quality to algorithm |
| Comments per post | 15+ | Drives engagement score |
| Profile visits | Growing week over week | Means hooks are working |
| Follower growth rate | 2-5% per week in growth phase | Sustainable, compound growth |
| DMs per week | 10+ | Conversion pipeline is alive |

## Summary: The System

1. **Study outliers.** Find what's already working in adjacent niches. Use the `find-outliers` skill to log them.
2. **Write hooks using the formula.** Pain surface + emotional truth + open loop.
3. **Post from the five pillars.** Mirror (40%), Reframe (25%), Story (15%), Practice (10%), Spark (10%).
4. **Engineer shareability.** Every post should pass the "would she send this to her best friend?" test.
5. **Optimise for saves, shares, and watch time.** These are the algorithm signals that drive reach.
6. **Use keyword CTAs.** Drive comments and DM automation simultaneously.
7. **Repurpose everything.** One idea, five formats. Never waste a performing hook.
8. **Track what matters.** Saves, shares, watch time, DMs, email signups. Weekly.
