# Content Prompts

A reusable bank of prompts to paste into Claude for building, packaging, and
shipping content fast.

---

## 2. Content strategy prompt

Paste this into Claude:

You are my content strategist.

My niche is [insert niche].
My audience is [insert audience].
My goal is [growth, leads, sales, authority].

Give me 5 content pillars that would make people follow, trust, and buy from me.

For each pillar, give me 10 post ideas.

---

## 3. Viral angle prompt

Paste this:

Turn this topic into 10 scroll stopping content angles:

[insert topic]

For each angle, give me the hook, the emotion it triggers, why people would care, the best visual idea, and the first comment idea.

Make the angles simple, useful, and easy to understand fast.

---

## 4. Hook prompt

Paste this:

Write 25 hooks for this post idea:

[insert idea]

Rules:
Keep them short, human, and punchy.
Use curiosity, pain, surprise, or speed.
Avoid generic AI phrases.
Do not make fake claims.

Rank the best 5.

---

## 5. Caption prompt

Paste this:

Write a short social caption for this idea:

[insert idea]

Style:
Direct, human, punchy, no fluff, no robotic words.

Maximum 4 lines.

End with:
Here is exactly how 👇🏽👇🏽

Give me 5 versions.

---

## 6. Comment guide prompt

Paste this:

Turn this post into a useful comment guide:

[insert caption]

Give me numbered comments.

Each comment should teach one practical idea.

Make it simple, useful, and ready to paste.

Include prompts, steps, or examples only when needed.

---

## 7. Visual prompt

Paste this:

Give me 3 visual concepts for this post:

[insert caption]

Rules:
One clear hero object, black background, high contrast, thumbnail style, easy to understand in one second, no clutter, no tiny icons.

Pick the strongest concept and write the full image prompt.

---

## 8. Repurpose prompt

Paste this after the post is done:

Turn this post into 6 formats:

1. Facebook text post
2. Carousel outline
3. Reel script
4. Tweet thread
5. LinkedIn post
6. Story sequence

Keep the same idea but change the format for each platform.

---

## 9. Brutal editor prompt

Paste this before posting:

Act like a brutal content editor.

Review this post:

[paste post]

Tell me what is boring, what is confusing, what should be cut, and what would make it more shareable.

Then rewrite it better.
