# From Roommates to Lovers Membership: Pricing & Value Perception

Hormozi-style pricing for the $97/month membership, calibrated to Tarisha's audience.

---

## Context

- **Offer:** From Roommates to Lovers, the membership. Monthly live teach with Tarisha, fresh 7-day audio practice each month for couples, growing Lovers Deck library, private members' space, optional live Q&A capped at 50 couples a month.
- **Audience:** women 40+, 10+ years in, more love than spark, partners mostly willing if invited well.
- **Result delivered:** the couple reaches for each other again. Sex back on the table. The fight stops repeating. Touch in the kitchen lands.
- **Time to result:** noticeable shift in seven days. New pattern set inside ninety days. Compounding from month three.

---

## 1. Perceived value, mapped on the four Hormozi levers

| Lever | Reality for this offer | Strength |
|---|---|---|
| **Dream outcome** | A marriage that has sex, laughter, and reach in it for the next 30 to 40 years. Avoiding the slow divorce, the affair, the decade of bedroom roommates. | Very high. This is the most important relationship in her life. |
| **Perceived likelihood** | High if both engage (decade of clinical practice behind the method). Medium-high if she starts solo (proof: many of the existing $97 buyers began alone). | High, once we show proof. |
| **Time delay** | First shift inside seven days. New pattern inside ninety days. | Fast for the category. |
| **Effort & sacrifice** | Fifteen minutes a day, one week a month. Audio-led, no homework. | Low. |

**Net:** dream is huge, likelihood is provable, delay is short, effort is small. The maths says this is wildly underpriced at $97/month. Our job is to make her feel that before she clicks.

## 2. How we raise perceived value before checkout

**Certainty (raise the likelihood number in her head):**

- Tarisha's credentials, named on the page: Master's in Counselling, EFT Levels 1 + 2, Hakomi, Gottman, NZAC, decade of clinical practice.
- Three short stories from couples who started solo, partner came in by week three.
- The First-Week Promise: complete Days 1 to 3, if you don't feel a shift in the room, full refund and you keep the Lovers Deck.
- A visible "what shifts when" timeline (Day 1, Day 7, Month 3) so she can see her own future.

**Reduce time delay (shorten the loop she imagines):**

- Day 1 practice unlocks the moment she joins.
- "Tonight" is the operative word on the CTA, not "this week".
- Live monthly teach is scheduled the week she joins, not a month out.

**Reduce effort (done-for-you and done-with-you):**

- Audio-led: she presses play, the practice runs itself.
- The Best Sex Talk script, word for word.
- The Reach For Him script, three openers she can text him today.
- The Cycle Map, fill-in-the-blank, twenty minutes.

Each of these moves the perceived value up without touching the price.

## 3. Pricing position, against the only two anchors that matter

**Cost of not solving:**

- A separation in New Zealand: $15,000 to $40,000 in legal fees, plus the house, plus the years.
- The affair she'd hate herself for: priceless on the wrong side.
- Another decade of polite: the cost of a life half-lived. No number on that, and she knows it.
- One year of $97 a month is $1,164. One round of couples therapy is $2,400 to $3,600 and most therapists don't touch sex.

**Alternative solutions:**

| Alternative | Cost | Why it doesn't hold |
|---|---|---|
| Couples therapy in Auckland | $220 to $300 a session, $2,400+ a course | Talks the emotional pattern, leaves the sexual one |
| A weekend couples retreat | $1,500 to $5,000 | One hit, no follow-through, the pattern returns |
| Books and podcasts | $30 to $100 a year | Information, no practice container |
| Doing nothing | $0 cash, life cost compounding | The pattern hardens |
| **This membership** | **$97/month, cancel any time** | **Works both patterns, every month, in fifteen minutes a day** |

Position line: "Less than half of one therapy session, with practice you actually finish, every month."

## 4. The three tiers

### Tier 1, entry: The Lovers Deck. $27, one-time.

- 40 printable conversation cards.
- For the quiz-taker who is curious but not yet ready to commit.
- Functions as the buyer's-list builder. Job: convert subscribers into buyers, which is the precondition for the membership.
- Easy yes. Pizza-night money.

### Tier 2, core: The Membership. $97/month.

- Live monthly 90-minute teach with Tarisha (recorded for life).
- Fresh 7-day audio-led practice each month for the two of you.
- Growing Lovers Deck library, new cards each month.
- Private members' space with the women walking the same road.
- Live Q&A capped at 50 couples a month.
- Cancel any time, two clicks, no email.
- This is the revenue engine. Recurring, compounding, fits Tarisha's "move away from per-hour work" line in ME.md.

### Tier 3, premium: The Couples Intensive. $2,400, one quarter at a time.

- Membership included for the quarter.
- Three private 90-minute sessions with Tarisha and Mark (one each month).
- Voxer access to Tarisha between sessions.
- A bespoke pattern map for your specific cycles.
- Capped at 10 couples per quarter so it stays personal.
- High margin (about 6 hours of Tarisha and Mark's time per couple, plus included assets).
- Natural feeder to the $12K Love Adventure retreat: a couple finishes the intensive, the retreat is the next step they want.

**Ladder logic:** $27 → $97/month → $2,400/quarter → $12K retreat. Each step is a "no-brainer" relative to the next. No tier exists for its own sake. Each one moves the buyer toward the room she actually wants to be in.

## 5. Why this pricing is logical AND emotional

**Logical:**

- $97/month is less than half a single therapy session. The buyer can do the maths in her head.
- The 14-day shift-or-refund pulls the risk off her and puts it on Tarisha.
- The library compounds: month six gets you six months of teaches, the price did not change.
- Cancel any time means the worst-case cost is one month, $97. She has spent that on a single dinner that went nowhere.

**Emotional:**

- $97 is "I am worth this" money. Not "I am desperate" money. The price signals dignity, not rescue.
- Monthly recurring means she does not have to decide once and forever. She decides one month at a time. The pressure is off.
- "Cancel in two clicks" tells her she is not being trapped. The membership has to earn her every month.
- The tier above ($2,400) makes $97 feel like the sensible, modest choice. It is.
