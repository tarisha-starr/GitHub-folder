# Step 1 — 10 Digital Product Ideas

**Strategist brief:** You run the *Roommates to Lovers* membership ($97/mo). Your
audience is women 40+ in long-term relationships and marriages who feel more like
roommates than lovers — the spark, sex, touch, and closeness have faded and they
want it back without nagging, awkwardness, or dragging a reluctant partner to
therapy. You already create emotionally resonant content on desire, intimacy,
body, and midlife.

Every idea below is built to **create a quick result and feed the membership.**

> Format key for "Difficulty": 1 = write it in an afternoon · 10 = weeks of production.

---

## The 10 ideas

### 1. The 7-Day Reconnection Reset  ⭐ TOP PICK
1. **Target buyer:** Woman 40+ who feels like a roommate, not a lover, and wants change *this week*.
2. **Painful problem:** Months/years of distance; she doesn't know where to start and it feels too big.
3. **Format:** PDF/Notion guide + 7 short daily emails + 3 short audio practices.
4. **Why they pay:** It removes the overwhelm — one tiny, doable move per day with the exact words to use.
5. **Fastest version:** 7 one-page daily prompts + a one-line script for each. Ship today.
6. **Easiest marketing:** "I dare you to try this for 7 days" challenge post → email opt-in.
7. **Price:** $19–$39 (tripwire to the $97/mo membership).
8. **Difficulty:** 2/10.

### 2. The Initiation Scripts
1. **Buyer:** Women who freeze up — don't know *what to say* to reconnect or initiate.
2. **Problem:** Fear of rejection and awkwardness kills the attempt before it starts.
3. **Format:** Swipe file of 50+ exact texts/lines for affection, flirting, initiating, repairing.
4. **Why they pay:** Removes the hardest part — finding the words in the moment.
5. **Fastest version:** A 10-page categorized script bank.
6. **Marketing:** "The text that restarts desire" carousel.
7. **Price:** $17–$27.
8. **Difficulty:** 2/10.

### 3. Roommates-to-Lovers Quiz + Custom Roadmap
1. **Buyer:** Women unsure how far gone things are and what to do first.
2. **Problem:** No clarity, no starting point, lots of shame.
3. **Format:** Scored quiz (e.g. Typeform) → tailored PDF roadmap by result type.
4. **Why they pay:** Personalized diagnosis feels made-for-me; great list builder.
5. **Fastest version:** 12-question quiz, 3 result archetypes, 1 roadmap each.
6. **Marketing:** "Are you in the roommate phase? Find out in 2 minutes."
7. **Price:** Free lead magnet OR $9 → membership.
8. **Difficulty:** 4/10.

### 4. Date Night In A Box (Digital)
1. **Buyer:** Couples stuck in logistics mode; "date night" = takeout and screens.
2. **Problem:** Time together exists but connection doesn't.
3. **Format:** Printable kit — conversation cards, a touch ritual, a playlist, a no-screens plan.
4. **Why they pay:** Done-for-you romance with zero planning.
5. **Fastest version:** One printable date night + 20 conversation cards.
6. **Marketing:** Aesthetic flat-lay reel of the printed kit.
7. **Price:** $19–$29 (or a monthly drop inside the membership).
8. **Difficulty:** 3/10.

### 5. The Resentment Release Journal
1. **Buyer:** Women whose desire is buried under built-up resentment and mental load.
2. **Problem:** You can't want someone you quietly resent — this is the real blocker.
3. **Format:** Guided 14-day journal with prompts to name, release, and re-see her partner.
4. **Why they pay:** Names the unspoken reason the spark died.
5. **Fastest version:** 14 prompts + short framing intros.
6. **Marketing:** "Why you stopped wanting him (it's not what you think)."
7. **Price:** $19–$29.
8. **Difficulty:** 3/10.

### 6. The Touch Reset (Audio Series)
1. **Buyer:** Couples where all touch stopped, even non-sexual; bed feels like a border.
2. **Problem:** Touch became loaded, so they avoid it entirely.
3. **Format:** 5 short guided audio practices rebuilding touch from hand-holding up.
4. **Why they pay:** Gentle, pressure-free, "press play" simplicity.
5. **Fastest version:** 5 audios (3–6 min) + a one-page guide.
6. **Marketing:** "The 60-second touch that changes the whole night."
7. **Price:** $27–$39.
8. **Difficulty:** 4/10 (light recording).

### 7. The 90-Minute Reconnection Workshop (Recorded)
1. **Buyer:** Women ready to invest an evening to understand and fix the pattern.
2. **Problem:** They want the "why" and a real plan, not just tips.
3. **Format:** Recorded video workshop + workbook.
4. **Why they pay:** Depth and a framework; strong membership on-ramp.
5. **Fastest version:** Slides + screen recording + 4-page workbook.
6. **Marketing:** Free live masterclass → replay → membership pitch.
7. **Price:** $47–$97.
8. **Difficulty:** 5/10.

### 8. Connection Card Deck (Printable + Digital)
1. **Buyer:** Couples who want easy, repeatable closeness without "a talk."
2. **Problem:** Conversations default to logistics; intimacy needs a prompt.
3. **Format:** 52 cards across Reconnect / Flirt / Desire / Dream tiers.
4. **Why they pay:** Reusable, giftable, low-effort intimacy.
5. **Fastest version:** 52 cards as a print-ready PDF.
6. **Marketing:** "Pull one card a night for 30 days" challenge.
7. **Price:** $19–$29.
8. **Difficulty:** 3/10.

### 9. Bedroom Confidence Guide (Body + Desire, Midlife)
1. **Buyer:** Women 40+ who avoid intimacy because they feel disconnected from their body.
2. **Problem:** Self-consciousness about a changing body shuts desire down.
3. **Format:** Guide reframing desirability + practices to feel at home in her body.
4. **Why they pay:** Speaks the unsaid block behind avoidance.
5. **Fastest version:** 15-page guide + 5 affirmation/practice prompts.
6. **Marketing:** "Desire doesn't have an age limit" emotional post.
7. **Price:** $27–$39.
8. **Difficulty:** 4/10.

### 10. The Reignite Email Mini-Course (Free → Paid)
1. **Buyer:** Cold audience not ready to buy yet.
2. **Problem:** They're curious but unconvinced; need to trust you first.
3. **Format:** 5-day free email course → pitches the Reset / membership on day 5.
4. **Why they pay:** Free entry builds the list and warms buyers.
5. **Fastest version:** 5 emails you can write in a day.
6. **Marketing:** "Free 5-day Reignite series" opt-in everywhere.
7. **Price:** Free (list builder feeding $97/mo).
8. **Difficulty:** 2/10.
