# Step 3 — The Smallest Useful Version

**Product:** The 7-Day Desire Reset (women only)
**Buyer:** A woman 40+ who has lost touch with her own sexual desire and aliveness
— intimacy feels like a duty, her body feels like a stranger — and she wants to
feel *alive in herself* again, then bring that into her relationship.

You're teaching **embodiment first, relationship skills second.** Build the
version that creates a felt shift in *her*, not a couples' workbook.

## 1. The exact result the buyer should get
By the end of 7 days she has **felt desire and aliveness in her own body again**
— at least a few real moments of sensation, pleasure, and wanting that hadn't been
happening — and she has the relationship skills to begin bringing that into
intimacy. She feels reconnected to herself, not fixed-from-the-outside.

## 2. Product format
A short **PDF/Notion guide** (the spine) + **7 daily emails** (delivery + momentum)
+ **3 short embodiment audio practices** (guided, press-play). That's it for v1.

## 3. Sections / modules
- **Welcome & the one rule** (~1 page): this week is *for you*; you're not fixing the relationship, you're coming home to yourself.
- **The Reset Map** (1 page): the 7 days at a glance (body → desire → senses → body-peace → pleasure → intimacy → ongoing practice).
- **Days 1–7**, each one page: today's practice + an embodiment cue + why it works (2 lines) + a nightly noticing prompt.
- **Day 7 → What's next:** the invitation into the women-only membership.

## 4. What MUST be included
- One short, private embodiment or desire practice per day (10–15 min).
- A concrete **body cue** so it's *felt*, not just read.
- A 2-line "why this works" so she trusts the process.
- A clear safety note (trauma, pain, unsafe relationship → see a professional).
- The bridge to the $97/mo membership.

## 5. What should be REMOVED (for v1)
- Partner worksheets, couples exercises, video lessons, a course platform, a
  community, anatomy deep-dives, a quiz. Embodiment-heavy theory. Add later.

## 6. The order the buyer follows
Body awareness → desire as hers → senses → body-peace → her own pleasure →
bringing it into intimacy → ongoing practice. **Self first, relationship last** —
that order is the whole philosophy.

## 7. Completion checklist (for her)
- [ ] Read the welcome and the one rule
- [ ] Did the daily practice Days 1–7
- [ ] Actually did the body practices (not just read them)
- [ ] Listened to the 3 audios
- [ ] Wrote my nightly "what did I notice in my body" line
- [ ] Chose my keystone practice + next 30 days (membership)

## 8. Final deliverables
1. `The 7-Day Desire Reset` guide (PDF + Notion).
2. 7 scheduled daily emails.
3. 3 embodiment audio practices (4–7 min each).
4. A one-page "what's next" membership invite.

## 9. The fastest version I can build TODAY
The full guide is already written in `04-the-product.md`. To launch today: paste
into a clean Google Doc → export PDF, drop the 7 emails into your email tool,
record the 3 embodiment audios on your phone, and sell with a checkout link. **No
course platform needed.**

## 10. The better version to add later (v2+)
A desire/embodiment quiz front-end, longer guided audio library, short
demonstration videos for the body practices, a private women-only community, and a
deeper "erotic confidence" track — **only after** real buyers tell you what they
needed more of.
