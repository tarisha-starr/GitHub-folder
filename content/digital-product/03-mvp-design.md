# Step 3 — The Smallest Useful Version

**Product:** The 7-Day Reconnection Reset
**Buyer:** Women 40+ who feel like roommates and want to feel close again — fast,
privately, and without a big confrontation.

Build the version that creates a *result*, not the version that looks impressive.

## 1. The exact result the buyer should get
By the end of 7 days she has had **at least three real moments of warmth or
desire** with her partner that hadn't been happening — and proof that *she* can
restart connection without a big talk or his buy-in. She feels hopeful and in
motion instead of stuck.

## 2. Product format
A short **PDF/Notion guide** (the spine) + **7 daily emails** (delivery + momentum)
+ **3 short audio practices** (press-play ease). That's it for v1.

## 3. Sections / modules
- **Welcome & the one rule** (~1 page): how it works, "start tonight, alone is fine."
- **The Reset Map** (1 page): the 7 days at a glance.
- **Days 1–7**, each one page: today's tiny move + the exact words + why it works (2 lines).
- **Day 7 → What's next:** the invitation into the membership.

## 4. What MUST be included
- One small, doable action per day (under 15 min).
- The **exact script/words** for each day (this is the core value).
- A 2-line "why this works" so she trusts it.
- A safety note (when to seek a professional instead).
- A clear next step to the $97/mo membership.

## 5. What should be REMOVED (for v1)
- Video lessons, a course platform, a community, workbooks, a quiz, partner
  worksheets, bonus PDFs, design polish beyond clean and readable. Add later.

## 6. The order the buyer follows
Welcome → Day 1 (warmth) → 2 (appreciation) → 3 (touch) → 4 (play/flirt) →
5 (curiosity) → 6 (desire) → 7 (vision + next step). Emotional → physical →
forward-looking, the same arc therapists describe (non-sexual touch returns first).

## 7. Completion checklist (for her)
- [ ] Read the welcome and the one rule
- [ ] Did Day 1–7, one move each day
- [ ] Used at least one script word-for-word
- [ ] Listened to the 3 audios
- [ ] Noted the moments that landed
- [ ] Decided my next 30 days (membership)

## 8. Final deliverables
1. `The 7-Day Reconnection Reset` guide (PDF + Notion).
2. 7 scheduled daily emails.
3. 3 audio files (3–6 min each).
4. A one-page "what's next" membership invite.

## 9. The fastest version I can build TODAY
The full guide is already written in `04-the-product.md`. To launch today:
paste it into a clean Google Doc → export PDF, drop the 7 emails into your email
tool, record the 3 audios on your phone, and sell it with a checkout link.
**No course platform needed.**

## 10. The better version to add later (v2+)
Quiz/roadmap front end (#3), the Initiation Scripts bump (#2), short videos, a
printable card deck (#8), a private community, and partner-optional bonus tracks —
**only after** real buyers tell you what they wished it had.
