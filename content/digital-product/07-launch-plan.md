# Step 7 — 7-Day Launch Plan

**Launching:** The 7-Day Reconnection Reset ($27 → $97/mo membership).
**Audience:** Women 40+ who feel like roommates, not lovers.
**Main platform:** Instagram (Reels + Stories), mirrored to email. One-person doable.

> Strategy: 3 days warming the audience (problem/story/teach), then open the cart,
> then handle objections, then a gentle close. Sell from *empathy and proof*, not
> hype or fake countdowns.

## Daily plan

### Day 1 — Name the pain (warm-up, no pitch)
1. **Angle:** "It's not broken. You're roommates." Make her feel seen.
2. **Hook:** "You don't hate him. You just can't remember the last time you felt like *lovers.*"
3. **Post:** Reel over a quiet domestic b-roll; list the silent signs of roommate mode.
4. **CTA:** "Comment 'me' if this is hitting too close." (engagement only)
5. **Format:** Reel + Story poll: "Do you feel more like roommates or lovers lately?"
6. **Objection handled:** "I'm the only one." (No — it's incredibly common.)
7. **Proof:** Reference that even the Gottman Institute named this exact pattern.

### Day 2 — Story (build trust)
1. **Angle:** Your own / a client's roommate-phase turning point.
2. **Hook:** "We slept in the same bed for two years and felt like strangers. Here's the night that changed."
3. **Post:** Story-driven carousel or talking Reel.
4. **CTA:** "Save this if you needed to hear it." + "DM me 'RESET' to hear what I'm opening."
5. **Format:** Carousel (5–7 slides) or face-to-camera Reel.
6. **Objection:** "It's too late for us." (It wasn't for me/her.)
7. **Proof:** Your transformation; a line from someone you've helped.

### Day 3 — Teach (give real value)
1. **Angle:** The right *order* of reconnection — warmth before desire.
2. **Hook:** "Stop trying to fix the bedroom first. That's why it's not working."
3. **Post:** Teach the warmth → touch → desire sequence (mini version of the product).
4. **CTA:** "Tomorrow I'm opening something that walks you through all 7 days. Turn on notifications."
5. **Format:** Carousel + Story teaser of the cover.
6. **Objection:** "Generic advice never works." (This is specific and ordered.)
7. **Proof:** Explain *why* the order works (non-sexual touch returns first).

### Day 4 — OPEN THE CART
1. **Angle:** Introduce the Reset + the offer.
2. **Hook:** "It's here: 7 nights to go from roommates to lovers — without a single awkward 'we need to talk.'"
3. **Post:** Reveal Reel — what it is, who it's for, the promise, the price.
4. **CTA:** "Link in bio — The 7-Day Reconnection Reset, $27."
5. **Format:** Announcement Reel + Stories walkthrough (swipe-up/link) + email.
6. **Objection:** "Will this actually be different?" (Done-for-you scripts, start tonight, solo.)
7. **Proof:** Show the cover, the day titles, the refund guarantee.

### Day 5 — Handle the #1 objection
1. **Angle:** "But my husband won't participate."
2. **Hook:** "You don't need his buy-in. You don't even need to tell him."
3. **Post:** Explain the solo-startable design; connection is contagious.
4. **CTA:** "Start tonight, alone — link in bio."
5. **Format:** Face-to-camera Reel + Story Q&A box.
6. **Objection:** "He won't do it." (Every step is yours to lead.)
7. **Proof:** A buyer screenshot, or "X women started this week."

### Day 6 — Bedroom-order myth + desire
1. **Angle:** Reassure it's not "just sex stuff," and desire is allowed at 40+.
2. **Hook:** "Desire after 40 isn't gone. It's just buried under the laundry."
3. **Post:** Reframe desire as warming the ground, not forcing a spark.
4. **CTA:** "Day 6 is desire — but it starts gentle. Link in bio."
5. **Format:** Reel + testimonial Story.
6. **Objection:** "I don't feel desire anymore." (You will — after warmth, touch, curiosity.)
7. **Proof:** A DM/comment from someone who felt a shift.

### Day 7 — Gentle close + bridge to membership
1. **Angle:** Recap + honest, no-pressure last call.
2. **Hook:** "One small move tonight, or one more quiet week. Your call — no pressure, just an honest question."
3. **Post:** Recap the week's posts; restate promise, guarantee, and what's next (membership).
4. **CTA:** "Get the Reset → and if you love it, keep going in the membership."
5. **Format:** Carousel recap + email + Stories.
6. **Objection:** "I'll do it later." (Later is how another year passes.)
7. **Proof:** Aggregate proof — buyers so far, screenshots, the guarantee.

## Keep it simple (one-person execution)
- Batch-film the 7 Reels in one afternoon; schedule them.
- Reuse each day's hook as the email subject line.
- Spend 15 min/day replying to comments and DMs — that's where sales close.
- Track only two numbers: link clicks and sales. Adjust the next day from what lands.

## After launch — version 2 (steps 1→7 are a loop)
Read your DMs and refund reasons. The most common question becomes a new product
or a sales-page section. Add the bump, the quiz front-end, or short videos *only*
after buyers ask. Launch fast, listen, make v2 better — don't build for 30 days
before knowing anyone wants it.
