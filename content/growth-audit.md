## Audit: Current Strategy Analysis

### What's working well

1. **The formula is strong.** Pain hook + emotional truth is the #1 format in this niche. The hooks are specific, raw, and speak directly to the audience's private experience. This is rare. Most creators default to generic affirmations.

2. **The voice is authentic.** No coaching cliches, no performative empathy. The style guide enforces conversational delivery, contractions, and NZ spelling. This creates a distinctive voice that's recognisable across posts.

3. **The content library is deep.** 28 image posts, hook bank, formula document, JSON-structured posts, journal prompts, infographics, testimonials, and book content. The infrastructure is built for scale.

4. **The automation pipeline exists.** Daily email, Buffer integration, Zapier pushes, scheduler. This is ahead of 95% of creators in the niche.

5. **The CTA strategy is built in.** Keyword CTAs ("comment 'me'", "comment 'connection'") enable both engagement and DM automation.

---

### What's limiting growth

#### 1. Over-reliance on image posts
**Problem:** The current content library is image-heavy (static posts with text overlays). In 2025-2026, Instagram's algorithm heavily favours Reels for discovery and reach. Image posts serve existing followers but don't bring in new audiences.

**Fix:** Shift to 60% Reels, 25% carousels, 15% image posts. Every image post hook should also be turned into a 15-second Reel script.

#### 2. No Reels in the pipeline
**Problem:** The automation pipeline handles images and captions but has no Reel workflow. The `videos/` folder has 28 videos but no clear mapping to hooks, scripts, or posting strategy.

**Fix:** Build a Reel production system:
- Map each hook to a Reel script (talking head, text-on-screen, or b-roll)
- Use the `reel-scripts.md` file as a starting template
- Batch-film 10-15 Reels in one session, edit, schedule

#### 3. No carousel content
**Problem:** Carousels are the #1 save-driving format on Instagram. They also get shared as screenshots and bookmarked. The current library has zero carousels.

**Fix:** Create carousel templates for:
- "3 signs your body..." (list format)
- "What [pain] actually looks like vs what you think" (comparison)
- Mini-practices (step-by-step)
- The reframe (slide 1: the lie / slide 2: the truth)

#### 4. Posting frequency may be too low
**Problem:** With 28 posts on rotation, the content recycles monthly. The algorithm rewards consistency and novelty. Repeat content gets suppressed.

**Fix:** Target 7 posts/week minimum. Use the "1 idea, 5 formats" rule:
- Take a top hook → image post + Reel + carousel + Story series + email
- This 5x's the content output without needing new ideas

#### 5. No trending/timely content
**Problem:** All content is evergreen. Evergreen is good for saves and longevity, but trending content (trending audio, meme formats, cultural moments) is what drives discovery spikes.

**Fix:** Dedicate 10-15% of posts to trending formats. Every Saturday, post a trending-audio Reel with a niche-specific hook. Use the "Spark" pillar from the strategy.

#### 6. No collaboration or cross-pollination
**Problem:** Growth in isolation is slow. The fastest-growing creators in this niche actively collaborate: joint Lives, carousel takeovers, podcast swaps, comment engagement.

**Fix:** Identify 10 complementary creators (pelvic floor physios, menopause nutritionists, couples therapists, midlife fitness coaches). Engage daily on their content. Propose one collaboration per month.

#### 7. No Story strategy
**Problem:** Stories keep existing followers engaged and drive DMs. There's no documented Story strategy.

**Fix:** Post 3-5 Stories daily:
- Morning: share a post or practice
- Midday: behind-the-scenes, personal moment, or poll
- Evening: engagement sticker (question, poll, slider)
- After posting: reshare the post to Stories with a "save this" CTA

## Content Structure Improvements

### Hook improvements

**Current hooks (strong):** "You're not broken. You're exhausted." — This works. Keep it.

**Hooks to add:**
- More question hooks: "When did love become logistics?" — Questions create mental engagement.
- More "stop" hooks: "Stop trying to fix your desire." — Pattern interrupts stop the scroll.
- More numbered hooks: "3 things your body..." — Numbers create completion drive.
- More "unpopular opinion" hooks: Forces engagement (agree/disagree).

### Caption structure improvements

**Current:** Hook → reframe → question/CTA. This works.

**Add these variations:**
- **The list caption:** 3-5 signs/symptoms, each on its own line. Drives comments ("drop the number").
- **The story caption:** "She said... I said..." Client narrative that creates an arc.
- **The micro-caption:** Just the hook + one question. Nothing else. Minimalism signals confidence.

### Visual improvements

**Current:** Warm-toned lifestyle images of women over 40. Good.

**Add:**
- Text-heavy graphics for shareable quote posts (hook as the full graphic)
- Carousel-specific design templates (consistent swipe experience)
- Video thumbnails that match the image aesthetic but with a "play" visual cue
