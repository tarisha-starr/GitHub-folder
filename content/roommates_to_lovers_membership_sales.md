## FAQ

**Do we need to have done From Roommates to Lovers first?**
No. The membership stands alone. But couples who've done the programme get more from the calls faster, because the language is already in your body.

**What if my partner is hesitant?**
Most partners are. Show them this page. Or join and do the first month yourself. The practices are designed for two, but the calls work solo too. Most reluctant partners come around after seeing what changes in you.

**Can we just do one month?**
Yes. Cancel after 30 days if it's not landing. No questions, no guilt. But the couples who stay three months or more are the ones who say it changed their relationship. The shift is cumulative.

**What if we miss a live call?**
Every call is recorded. Watch it together that weekend. Or catch the next one. Two per month means you've got a second chance built in.

**Is this the same as the Radiant Woman Circle?**
No. The Radiant Woman Circle is for women, solo. This is for couples. But they share DNA: the practices here are built from the same body of work (somatic, nervous system, desire, pleasure) and adapted for the relationship space. If she's in the Circle and he's willing, this is where the work meets the partnership.

**How is this different from therapy?**
Therapy processes what happened. This practises what's possible. They complement each other beautifully. Many of our couples do both.

**Is this "tantric" or "spiritual"?**
Nothing chanted. Nothing burned. Clinical research + somatic practice + adventure. Not woo.
