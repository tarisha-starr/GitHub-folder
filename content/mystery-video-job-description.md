# Job Description: AI Mystery Video Producer (Faceless YouTube Channel)

## Role
AI Video Editor / Producer for a faceless YouTube mystery channel targeting couples over 40. You will receive a completed script, storyboard, and case file for each video. Your job is to turn them into finished, publish-ready Shorts and long-form videos using AI tools.

## What You'll Do
- Generate cinematic AI images for each scene using tools like Midjourney, Leonardo, or Flux
- Animate scenes using AI video tools (Runway, Kling, Veo, Sora, or similar)
- Generate or edit AI voiceover (ElevenLabs, PlayHT, or similar) — calm, intimate, female narrator tone
- Edit the final video: pacing, transitions, text overlays, sound design, music bed
- Export in YouTube Shorts format (9:16, 60-90s) and optionally long-form (16:9, 8-15 min)
- Maintain visual consistency across videos: same character look, same color grade, same world
- Deliver 3-5 finished Shorts per week (ramping to 5-7 over time)

## Tools You Must Know (at least 3 of these)
- AI image generation: Midjourney, Leonardo AI, Flux, DALL-E
- AI video generation: Runway Gen-3/4, Kling, Pika, Veo, Sora
- AI voiceover: ElevenLabs, PlayHT, Murf
- Video editing: CapCut Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, or Final Cut
- Basic audio: background music, SFX layering, pacing

## What You Don't Need to Do
- Write scripts (provided to you)
- Design storyboards (provided to you)
- Come up with ideas (provided to you)
- Show your face or record your voice

## Ideal Candidate
- Has produced at least 5 faceless YouTube videos (show samples)
- Understands retention editing: fast cuts, visual hooks, pattern interrupts
- Can maintain a dark, cinematic, emotionally grounded visual style
- Comfortable with mystery/relationship/emotional content (not horror, not true crime gore)
- Responsive on chat (Slack, Telegram, or WhatsApp) within business hours PHT
- Detail-oriented: consistent character design, matched lighting, no AI artifacts left in final cut

## Nice to Have
- Experience with EFT (Emotionally Focused Therapy) or relationship content
- Thumbnail design (Canva or Photoshop)
- Basic YouTube SEO: titles, descriptions, tags
- Experience scaling a faceless channel past 10K subscribers

## Deliverables Per Video
1. 8-12 AI-generated scene images (approved before animation)
2. Animated video clips per scene (3-8 seconds each)
3. Voiceover audio synced to script
4. Final edited video with music, SFX, text overlays, and transitions
5. Thumbnail (if applicable)

## Work Arrangement
- Part-time to start (15-20 hrs/week), scaling to full-time based on performance
- Paid weekly or bi-weekly
- Long-term role — this is a channel build, not a one-off project
- All work is remote
