# Retention Prompts

**Paste any of these above a draft to run it through a specific lens.**

---

## Prompt 1: Pacing Audit

Act like a ruthless content pacing editor. Read the content below and find every moment that drags, stalls, or lets the reader's attention wander. For each slow moment: (1) why it drags, (2) what to cut, (3) what to replace it with, (4) how to make the next line pull forward. Keep things moving like a conversation, not a lecture.

---

## Prompt 2: Payoff Audit

Act like a content retention strategist focused on payoff. Read the content below and check every promise against its payoff. For every hook, open loop, question, or setup, verify the resolution is (1) worth the wait, (2) emotionally landing, (3) specific, not vague. Flag any payoff that feels generic, obvious, or weaker than the setup. Replace weak payoffs with stronger, more specific ones.

---

## Prompt 3: Format Optimisation

Act like a short-form video strategist. Take the content below and restructure it for maximum retention on Instagram Reels. Apply: (1) text-on-screen in first frame, (2) hook in first 1.5 seconds, (3) micro-interrupt at 3 seconds, (4) no sentence longer than 12 words, (5) strategic pauses marked with [BEAT], (6) quotable end-card line. Keep the voice raw and warm, not produced.

---

## Prompt 4: MrBeast-Style Retention Review

Imagine you're reviewing this content the way MrBeast reviews YouTube intros. Your job: find the exact second a viewer would click away. For each drop-off risk: (1) what causes the drop, (2) what would keep her watching for 3 more seconds, (3) what curiosity device to insert. Score each section 1-10 on "would she keep watching?" Be brutal but constructive.

---

## Prompt 5: Curiosity Loop Check

Read the content below and verify it uses exactly 3 curiosity touches (no more, no less): Touch 1 at seconds 0-3 (hook opens a question), Touch 2 at seconds 3-8 (micro question, interrupt, or delayed payoff), Touch 3 in the final 2-3 seconds (open loop to caption or a line that lingers). Flag any reel with fewer than 2 or more than 4 curiosity touches. Suggest specific fixes.

---

## Prompt 6: Voice Consistency Check (Tarisha Starr)

Read the content below and check every line against the Tarisha Starr style guide. Flag: (1) any em-dashes or en-dashes, (2) missing contractions (cannot, do not, etc.), (3) American spellings (realize, favorite, color), (4) therapist-speak ("research shows," "studies suggest"), (5) coaching cliches ("queen energy," "I see you sister"), (6) stiff transitions ("Furthermore," "However"). The voice should sound like a friend at 11pm, not a coach on a stage.

---

## Prompt 7: Improve Pacing

Rebuild this content for better pacing. Make the opening faster. Cut anything that doesn't move the idea forward. Move the strongest point earlier. Add rhythm with shorter lines. Make each section feel like it earns the next one.

---

## Prompt 8: Strengthen the Payoff

Audit the payoff of this content. Tell me if the ending feels worth the attention I asked for. Then give me 5 stronger endings:

1. Practical ending
2. Emotional ending
3. Save-worthy ending
4. Comment-bait ending
5. Share-worthy ending

Make each one feel earned.

---

## Prompt 9: Turn One Idea Into 5 Formats

Take this content idea and turn it into 5 high-retention formats:

1. Short video script (Instagram Reel)
2. Facebook text post
3. Carousel outline (Instagram)
4. YouTube Short
5. X thread

For each format, give me the hook, structure, payoff, and CTA. Don't repeat the same wording across formats.

---

## Prompt 10: Final MrBeast-Style Review

Review my final version like a top retention editor. Tell me:

- Where people will stop caring
- What line needs to be cut
- What line should be moved earlier
- What moment needs more emotion
- What payoff is still weak

Then rewrite the final version with stronger retention.
