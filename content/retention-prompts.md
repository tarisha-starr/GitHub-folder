# Retention prompts

Reusable prompts for tightening content before it ships. Paste the prompt,
then paste the draft below it. Each prompt is self-contained.

---

## Prompt 5 — Improve pacing

Rebuild this content for better pacing.

Make the opening faster.

Cut anything that does not move the idea forward.

Move the strongest point earlier.

Add rhythm with shorter lines.

Make each section feel like it earns the next one.

---

## Prompt 6 — Strengthen the payoff

Audit the payoff of this content.

Tell me if the ending feels worth the attention I asked for.

Then give me 5 stronger endings:

1. Practical ending
2. Emotional ending
3. Save worthy ending
4. Comment bait ending
5. Share worthy ending

Make each one feel earned.

---

## Prompt 7 — Turn one idea into 5 formats

Take this content idea and turn it into 5 high retention formats:

1. Short video script
2. Facebook text post
3. Carousel outline
4. YouTube Short
5. X thread

For each format, give me the hook, structure, payoff, and CTA.

Do not repeat the same wording.

---

## Prompt 8 — Final MrBeast style review

Review my final version like a top retention editor.

Tell me:

- Where people will stop caring
- What line needs to be cut
- What line should be moved earlier
- What moment needs more emotion
- What payoff is still weak

Then rewrite the final version with stronger retention.
